Controls

Game Controls:
- Drag the ball to set the direction and power. Release to fire.
- Mouse scroll to zoom in and out
- Use the slider to adjust gravity.
- Use the buttons to add bumpers or boosters.
- Press R to reset ball.
- Press Q for a new map.
- Press Esc to show menu
